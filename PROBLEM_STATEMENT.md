# Al Friday Season 2 - Problem Statements  
## Automated Banking FAQ Chatbot for Real-Time Customer Support  

### PROBLEM STATEMENT  
Bank customers often experience frustration due to delays and inconsistent answers when seeking information about account statuses, loan eligibility, and recent transactions, Traditional call centers handle large volumes of routine queries, resulting in extended wait times and variable response quality. This inefficiency not only decreases customer satisfaction but also inflates operational costs for banks. Currently, customer inquiries rely heavily on human agents referencing multiple resources, leading to delays and errors. There is a pressing need for an automated solution that can accurately interpret diverse, natural language questions and deliver real-time, precise responses without human involvement. Such a system must handle a wide range of banking topics, ensuring compliance with policies while maintaining fast, reliable service. Implementing this solution load, and improve overall operational efficiency. can transform customer engagement, reduce support  

### Data Considerations (Guidelines):  
- Curated FAQ datasets covering banking products, interest rates, and transaction processes in structured text;  
- anonymized sample customer queries from call logs reflecting natural language variations;  
- publicly available banking policy and product documents for reference;  
- synthetic data generation to augment training examples and simulate diverse queries;  
- data cleansing to remove PlIl and sensitive information to ensure compliance;  
- preprocessing to normalize question formats and extract key entities;  
- dataset volume sufficient to cover major banking topics without overwhelming prototype scope;  
- integration of data in JSON or CSV formats for easy ingestion.  

Feel free to generate synthetic data where appropriate using the provided environment  

### SOLUTION EXPECTATIONS  
A functional Al chatbot prototype that accepts natural language questions via a simple web interface and returns accurate, context-aware answers from banking FAQ data; core capabilities include intent recognition, entity extraction, and response generation using GenAl; technical deliverables include working codebase, usage guide, and demo video illustrating smooth, realistic user interactions; measurable outcomes focus on response accuracy above 85% and sub-3-second average reply time; demonstration highlights handling varied queries and maintaining answer consistency; basic APl integration supports future data updates; scalability options include modular FAQ expansion.  

Be creative to go beyond as you solve the above needs
